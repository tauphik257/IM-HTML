ABOUT PROGRAM

Over past few years with the beginning of digital era, we have been witnessing an exponential growth is data and is definitely not going to slow down soon. Data Science is evolving as a new domain and acting as an enabler for all business houses to take data driven decision.

IMT CDL and EY, with years of leadership in their respective domain, offering a 10 months Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Business Intelligence for those who wish to equip themselves for the new career opportunity. The program will help you to deep dive into Data Architecture, Python, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Visualization and Blockchain.
