about program


Over past few years with the beginning of digital era, we have been witnessing an exponential growth is data and is definitely not slow down soon. Data Science is evolving as a
new domain and acting as an enabler for all business houses to take data driven decision.

IMT CDL and EY, with years of leadership in their respective domain, offering a 10 months Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Business Intelligence for those who wish to equip
themselves for the new career opportunity. The program will help you to deep dive into Data Architecture, Python, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Visualization and
Blockchain.

Program Feature


  • PGP DSBI covers around 200 hours of VILT learning spread across 10 months.
  • The students / participants will be provided with 2 projects during the course.
    The students / participants shall be provided options to choose the projects from their respective domain/industry.
  • There will be a preliminary test conducted for the interested students. Those who clears the test shall be provided with the offer to join the program.
  • The program will be delivered by EY professionals and Industry experts to provide the practical insights of the real world problems.
  • The certification will have the weightage of the score achieved on the projects and final assessment.
  • *Internship opportunity with EY at Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore & Hyderabad location.
